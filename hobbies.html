My Hobbies

Reading

I have loved reading from a young age because it provides me the perfect opportunity to escape reality and put myself in another worlds experience.

I use reading as a wonderful stress reliever, a way to remind myself my reality doesn't have to be the only reality I can experience!

Working out

The gym and exercise in general is one of my favorite outlets. I love the idea of bettering my body and mind at the same time.

It has also been a great bonding point between me and my parents so it holds a special place in my heart.

Art

While this is my hobby I do the least, I love finding new ways to express myself and art never fails to provide that opportunity.

I love having an outlet where I can challenge myself to get better everytime because I don't practice as often.
